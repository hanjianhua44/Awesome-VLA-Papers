### 🔥 #1｜CodeGraphVLP: Code-as-Planner Meets Semantic-Graph State for Non-Markovian Vision-Language-Action Models

📎 [2604.22238](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22238) | `cs.RO`

> Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models promise generalist robot manipulation, but are typically trained and deployed as short-horizon policies that assume the latest observation is sufficient for action reasoning. This assumption breaks in non-Markovian long-horizon tasks, where task-relevant evidence can be occluded or appear only earlier in the trajectory, and where clutter and distractors make fine-grained visual grounding brittle.
