### 🔥 #1｜RotVLA: Rotational Latent Action for Vision-Language-Action Model

**Qiwei Li, Xicheng Gong, Xinghang Li 等 (8 位作者)** |  `Xiaomi, PKU, CASIA`

📎 [2605.13403](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13403) | `cs.RO cs.CV`

> Latent Action Models (LAMs) have emerged as an effective paradigm for handling heterogeneous datasets during Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model pretraining, offering a unified action space across embodiments. However, existing LAMs often rely on discrete quantization encode and decode pipelines, which can lead to trivial frame reconstruction behavior, limited representational capacity, and a lack of physically meaningful structure. We introduce RotVLA, a VLA framework built on a continuous rotational latent action representation. Latent actions are modeled as elements of SO(n), providing continuity, compositionality, and structured geometry aligned with real-world action dynamics. A triplet frame learning framework further enforces meaningful temporal dynamics while avoiding degeneration
>
> **关键技术：** 量化 / VLA / 预训练
