📎 [2605.30795](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30795) | `cs.RO`

> Reinforcement learning is a promising approach for improving the capabilities of vision-language-action (VLA) models while avoiding the heavy data requirements of imitation learning. In this work, we present Feat2Go, a fine-grained value estimation framework for embodied reinforcement learning. ### 🔥 #5｜ELAN4D: Embodiment-Centric 4D Supervision for Vision-Language-Action Models via Plug-and-Play Adaptation

📎 [2605.30484](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.30484) | `cs.RO`
