📎 [2606.23685](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.23685) | `cs.RO`

> While manual retargeting is indispensable for establishing kinematic action correspondence across different morphologies, robust transfer requires going beyond geometry to address the underlying alignment of physical dynamics between human and robot manipulation. ### 🔥 #10｜Cloak: Zero-Shot Cross-Embodiment Manipulation by Masking the End-Effector from the VLA

📎 [2606.22836](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.22836) | `cs.RO`

> We present Cloak, a training recipe that endows a Vision-Language-Action (VLA) model with zero-shot cross-embodiment transfer by cloaking the end-effector from its own wrist camera.
